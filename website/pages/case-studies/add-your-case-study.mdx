We would love to share your case study and use-case here!

Feel free to use the below template and open a PR to this [repository](https://github.com/cloudquery/cloudquery/new/main/website/pages/case-studies)

Alternatively, you can also share your use case in a less formal setting on the [Who's Using CloudQuery in Production](https://github.com/cloudquery/cloudquery/issues/856) section on GitHub.

## Introduction

Share the back story of why you needed a tool like CloudQuery, what you were looking to achieve and
what were some of the technical constraints.

## Architecture

Share the architecture of your deployment. Numbers, images and performance are always a great addition.

## Results

What were some of the results you achieved, and what are some of the things you are planning to explore further?
